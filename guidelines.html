Guidelines for Commissioning
Please read before sending in a request
  • I have the right to decline any request
  • I do not draw masculine characters, muscles, gore, violence, or heavy armor
  • You must pay before I begin working
  • Commissions can take two weeks or more to complete; please do not nag me ^^;
  • You can ask for minor changes or adjustments to the artwork
  • You are free to use your commissioned artwork in whatever manner you wish (with proper credit)
  • No refunds after I start drawing

    • If you have any inquiries, please feel free to contact me!
